Alex Carr

Alex Carr is a design leader with over 18 years of experience in interactive design, creative direction, and digital strategy. He is passionate about solving complex problems and creating enjoyable user experiences across industries and platforms. Most recently, Alex was the Chief Design Officer at WillowTree, where he led a global multidisciplinary design and content team delivering digital-first, branded experiences for clients such as T-Mobile, Marriott, PepsiCo, Domino's, and John Hancock/Manulife.

Before WillowTree, Alex was a Partner and SVP of Design at Maark, a strategic marketing and innovation agency, where he oversaw the creative vision and execution of digital transformation projects.

Alex is an alum of the Rhode Island School of Design, with a degree in Graphic Design. He is also a guest critic at Maine College of Art and Design.

If you're taking the T:
WillowTree is a 5 minute walk from Sullivan Square T-stop on the orange line at 40 Cambridge St, Charlestown, MA 02129.

If you're taking a taxi or car service:
Please tell them the 6 Spice Street address, and have the car pull around the building to the back lot to drop you off. (This is safer than getting dropped off on Cambridge Street.) WillowTree is on the left hand side. Signs will be posted; our garage door will be open. You can't miss us.
