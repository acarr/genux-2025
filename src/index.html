FAQ

For lunch we will provide vegetarian options. For further dietary restrictions requiring gluten free or vegan, or if you have food allergies, please email Linda Duong directly at Linda.Duong@willowtreeapps.com.

If you're taking the T:
WillowTree is a 5 minute walk from Sullivan Square T-stop on the orange line at 40 Cambridge St, Charlestown, MA 02129.

If you're taking a taxi or car service:
Please tell them the 6 Spice Street address, and have the car pull around the building to the back lot to drop you off. (This is safer than getting dropped off on Cambridge Street.) WillowTree is on the left hand side. Signs will be posted; our garage door will be open. You can't miss us.

If you're driving your own car, these are some good places to park:
